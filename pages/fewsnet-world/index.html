How to interpret this map

This map depicts food security ratings from FEWS NET (Famine Early Warning System) illustrating food insecurity in nations around the world. The date of the report shown in the data is in the upper left hand corner of the map. FEWS NET uses the IPC rating system.

IPC Phase 1: Minimal (Green)

IPC Phase 2: Stressed (Yellow)

IPC Phase 3: Crisis (Orange)

IPC Phase 4: Emergency (Red)

IPC Phase 5: Famine (Dark Red)
