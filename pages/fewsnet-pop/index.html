How to interpret this map

This map depicts food security ratings from FEWS NET (Famine Early Warning System) illustrating food insecurity in nations around the world. Choose the date of the report using the slider in the upper left of the menu. The circle colors reflect the rating given to that region. Use the style by menu to style the circle sizes by population or default circle sizes. Click on any rating dot on the map to see more information about that region and a historical graph of that regions food security.

FEWS NET uses the IPC rating system which illustrates food insecurity by color:

IPC Phase 1: Minimal (Green)
IPC Phase 2: Stressed (Yellow)
IPC Phase 3: Crisis (Orange)
IPC Phase 4: Emergency (Red)
IPC Phase 5: Famine (Dark Red)
